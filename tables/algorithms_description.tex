Adaptive Large Neighborhood Search (ALNS) & Destroys localized portions of a solution and rebuilds using multiple heuristics. & State-of-the-art framework; dynamically weights lower-level heuristics based on success. \\ \midrule

Ant Colony Optimization (ACO) & Constructive paths based on local heuristic info and global pheromone stigmergy. & Foundational swarm paradigm; rigorous value in probabilistic construction and stigmergy. \\ \midrule

Artificial Bee Colony (ABC) & Three agent types (Employed, Onlooker, Scout) model foraging. & \textbf{Deceptive.} Isomorphic to Differential Evolution mutation; often requires GA injections for discrete parity. \\ \midrule

Attention Model (AM) & Sequentially constructs a valid route by selecting vertices using a Transformer. & Foundational RL NCO baseline; successfully replaces hard-coded heuristics with learned policies. \\ \midrule

Attention Model with POMO & Forces the AM to start from multiple roots to exploit graph symmetry. & Highly effective training technique; mathematically sound exploitation of problem invariance. \\ \midrule

Branch-and-Bound (BB) & Systematic enumeration of candidate solutions via state-space search and pruning. & Foundational procedure for exact optimization; guaranteed optimality but computationally prohibitive at scale. \\ \midrule

Branch-and-Price-and-Cut (BPC) & Solves LP relaxations via simultaneous column and cut generation. & The gold standard for exact multicommodity flow; rigorously proves optimality via valid inequalities. \\ \midrule

Cluster-First Route-Second & Assigns customers via an exact GAP and sequences them heuristically. & Foundational decomposition; leverages exact solvers for spatial clustering where heuristics struggle. \\ \midrule

DeepACO & Uses a GNN trained via RL to output a matrix to initialize pheromones. & Excellent modern hybrid; uses DL to intelligently bias canonical swarm mathematics. \\ \midrule

Differential Evolution (DE) & Adds scaled differences between random members to a target base vector. & Highly effective continuous optimizer; the mathematical engine behind many relabeled swarm metaphors. \\ \midrule

Ensemble Move Acceptance & Combines multiple criteria (e.g., SA, GD, IE) for joint decisions. & Mitigates individual weaknesses; majority rule (G-VOT) is often the most robust. \\ \midrule

Genetic Programming Hyper-Heuristic (GPHH) & Evolves new heuristic rules using GP trees rather than searching solution space directly. & Powerful automated design paradigm; shifts the search space from solutions to algorithms. \\ \midrule

Great Deluge (GD) & Accepts moves better than a monotonically updating "water level" threshold. & Foundational deterministic alternative to SA; manages the exploration budget without stochasticity. \\ \midrule

Hybrid Genetic Search (HGS) & GA that applies local search to offspring prior to fitness evaluation. & State-of-the-art for routing; highly transparent and rigorous memetic engineering. \\ \midrule

Improving and Equal (IE) & Elitist strategy accepting candidates better than or equal to current solutions. & Crucial gatekeeper for navigating high levels of neutrality in combinatorial search spaces. \\ \midrule

Iterated Local Search (ILS) & Iterates local search by applying perturbation operators to escape local optima. & Foundational trajectory archetype utilizing intelligent mathematical restarts. \\ \midrule

Kernel Search (KS) & Restricts the set of active binary variables available to an iterative MILP solver. & Guarantees structural feasibility via exact solvers; superior to pure metaheuristics in tight polyhedra. \\ \midrule

Late Acceptance Hill-Climbing (LAHC) & Accepts candidates better than or equal to a solution from $L$ iterations ago. & Superior scale-independent memory mechanism; allows plateau traversal without recalibration. \\ \midrule

Learning-Augmented Matheuristics & Uses GNNs to score and predict branching outcomes, filtering candidates before MILP execution. & Traverses search trees exponentially faster by replacing LP solver tests with ML inference. \\ \midrule

Local Branching (VNSB) & Restricts MILP space by placing a bound on the Hamming distance from the incumbent. & Turns exact solvers into aggressive neighborhood explorers, pruning nodes instantaneously. \\ \midrule

Memetic Algorithm (MA) & Augments population-based search with intensive local trajectory refinement. & Foundational hybrid; bridges the gap between global exploration and local exploitation. \\ \midrule

Old Bachelor Acceptance (OBA) & Uses a non-monotone threshold that tightens on accept and loosens on reject. & Valid dynamic tuning schedule for SA/TA pipelines; prevents search stagnation. \\ \midrule

Only Improving (OI) & Accepts a move only if the objective cost is strictly better than the current. & Purely exploitative gatekeeper; defines the most basic elitist local search. \\ \midrule

POPMUSIC & Partitions massive routing instances into subproblems optimized exactly by a solver. & State-of-the-art decomposition; operates over large neighborhoods to escape local optima. \\ \midrule

Record-to-Record Travel (RRT) & Accepts moves within a fixed scalar margin of the best-known record. & Deterministic threshold rule; highly effective for sensitive sequences in cumulative VRPs. \\ \midrule

Relaxation Induced Neighborhood Search (RINS) & Fixes variables matching the LP relaxation and solves a restricted MILP. & Exploits commercial solvers as heuristic operators; effective for rapidly improving bounds. \\ \midrule

Restricted Master Heuristics & Replaces exact pricing with fast metaheuristics to generate columns for a Master Problem. & Overcomes column generation bottlenecks, establishing near-optimal bounds rapidly. \\ \midrule

Simulated Annealing (SA) & Probabilistically accepts worse moves based on Boltzmann distribution and temperature. & Foundational stochastic criterion; manages the exploration-exploitation balance through cooling. \\ \midrule

Step Counting Hill Climbing (SCHC) & Memory-based criterion where a cost bound is fixed for a set number of steps. & Scale-independent alternative to SA; robust performance on timetabling benchmarks. \\ \midrule

Threshold Accepting (TA) & Accepts deteriorating moves that fall within a predefined threshold range. & Deterministic alternative to SA; provides efficient reorganization of tour sequences. \\ \midrule

Variable Neighborhood Search (VNS) & Systematically switches neighborhood structures when trapped in local optima. & Foundational framework establishing rules for trajectory diversification. \\ \midrule

VRPP Exact Optimizer & Maximizes collection profits via a data-driven rolling horizon MILP model. & Exact solver implementation; utilizes HMMs to handle uncertainty while proving daily optimality. \\ \bottomrule
