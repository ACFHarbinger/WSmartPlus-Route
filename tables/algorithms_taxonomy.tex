Adaptive Large Neighborhood Search (ALNS) & Ropke \& Pisinger (2006) & LNS & Domain-Specific Meta-heuristic & Single-point, Routing & Ruin and Recreate & Destroys localized portions of a solution; rebuilds using multiple heuristics. & State-of-the-art routing framework; dynamically controls and weights multiple lower-level heuristics. \\ \midrule

Ant Colony Optimization (ACO) & Dorigo (1992) & Canonical & Canonical Meta-heuristic (Swarm) & Swarm, Combinatorial & Pheromone update & Constructive paths based on local heuristic info and global pheromone. & Foundational swarm paradigm; rigorous mathematical value in stigmergy and probabilistic construction. \\ \midrule

Artificial Bee Colony (ABC) & Karaboga (2005) & DE / ES & Relabeled Meta-heuristic (Swarm) & Swarm, Continuous & Vector mutation & Three agent types (Employed, Onlooker, Scout) model foraging. & \textbf{Deceptive.} Isomorphic to DE mutation and proportional selection. Often requires canonical GA crossover injections to remain competitive in discrete spaces. \\ \midrule

Attention Model (AM) & Kool et al. (2018) & Seq2Seq / REINFORCE & NCO: Autoregressive Constructive (RL) & Sequence, Combinatorial & Graph Attention, RL Policy & Sequentially constructs a valid route by selecting unvisited vertices using a Transformer. & Foundational RL NCO baseline. Replaces hard-coded heuristics with learned policies. \\ \midrule

Attention Model with POMO & Kwon et al. (2020) & AM + Rollouts & NCO: Autoregressive Constructive (RL) & Sequence, Combinatorial & Policy Optimization, Rollouts & Forces the AM to start from multiple roots, generating diverse trajectories to exploit symmetry. & Highly effective RL training technique; mathematically sound exploitation of graph symmetries. \\ \midrule

Branch-and-Price-and-Cut (BPC) & Laporte et al. (1998) & Branch-and-Bound & Exact Algorithm (Math. Prog.) & Complete Tree, Routing & Column gen., Cutting planes & Solves LP relaxations via pricing, tightens bounds with cuts, and branches for integrality. & The gold standard for exact optimization; rigorously proves global optimality without heuristics. \\ \midrule

Circuit-SAT GNN & Amizadeh et al. (2018) & GNN + DPLL & NCO: Predict-Many (UL) & Complete Tree, SAT / MILP & Unsupervised loss, DPLL & Iteratively predicts variable assignments to restrict the search tree via continuous circuit loss. & Purely mathematical Unsupervised Learning application for iterative state-space reduction. \\ \midrule

Deep Policy Dynamic Programming (DPDP) & Kool et al. (2022) & GNN + DP & NCO: Predict-Once (SL) & Complete Tree, Routing & Heatmap Gen., DP Bounds & SL model predicts edge probabilities (heatmaps) once to restrict the state space for exact DP. & Rigorously synthesizes DL probability predictions with exact, mathematically sound DP bounds. \\ \midrule

DeepACO & Ye et al. (2023) & GNN + ACO & NCO: Non-AR Constructive (RL) & Swarm, Combinatorial & Neural Pheromone Init. & Uses a GNN trained via RL to output a single non-autoregressive matrix to initialize pheromones. & Excellent modern hybrid. Uses DL to intelligently bias canonical swarm math. \\ \midrule

Differential Evolution (DE) & Storn \& Price (1997) & Canonical & Canonical Meta-heuristic & Population, Continuous & Vector difference mutation & Adds scaled differences between random members to a target base vector. & Highly effective continuous optimizer; the engine behind many modern "swarm" metaphors. \\ \midrule

Efficient Active Search (EAS) & Hottung et al. (2021) & AM + Active Search & NCO: Predict-Once (RL) & Sequence, Combinatorial & Test-time weight update & Predicts a baseline policy once, then uses RL at test-time to update the weights for the instance. & Novel Predict-Once RL application; blurs the line between inference and local trajectory search. \\ \midrule

Erdős Goes Neural (EGN) & Karalias \& Loukas (2020) & GNN + Decoding & NCO: Predict-Once (UL) & Single-point, Combinatorial & Probabilistic penalty loss & Unsupervised GNN outputs soft probabilities processed by sequential decoding for guarantees. & Rigorous bridging of DL probabilities with deterministic, discrete mathematical guarantees. \\ \midrule

Exact Learning to Branch (GNN-B\&B) & Gasse et al. (2019) & GNN + B\&B & NCO: Predict-Many (SL) & Complete Tree, MILP & GCN variable selection & Iteratively predicts branching variable probabilities at each node of the B\&B tree. & State-of-the-art Predict-Many framework. Replaces expensive branching calculations with GNNs. \\ \midrule

Fast Iterative Localized Optimization (FILO) & Accorsi et al. (2021) & ILS & Domain-Specific Heuristic & Single-point, Routing & Granular swap \& relocate & Local search constrained by predefined spatial granularity and proximity limits. & Genuinely effective spatial heuristic designed for massive-scale routing problems. \\ \midrule

Firefly Algorithm (FA) & Yang (2008) & PSO & Relabeled Meta-heuristic (Swarm) & Swarm, Continuous & Gaussian / random walk & Models bioluminescent attraction where brightness exponentially decays over distance. & \textbf{Deceptive.} A minor, continuous local-search derivative of PSO stripped of its velocity momentum terms; highly inefficient in complex combinatorial routing. \\ \midrule

Great Deluge (GD) & Dueck (1993) & Local Search (LS) & Canonical Acceptance Criterion & Single-point, Combinatorial & Relies on external LS & Accepts deteriorating moves if objective is better than a monotonically updating "water level". & Foundational deterministic alternative to Simulated Annealing. \\ \midrule

Harmony Search (HS) & Geem et al. (2001) & ES & Relabeled Meta-heuristic & Population, General & Pitch Adjustment (mutation) & Mimics musicians improvising to find acoustic harmony via pitch adjustments and memory. & \textbf{Deceptive.} Mathematically proven to be nothing more than a relabeled, constrained $(\mu + 1)$-Evolution Strategy (Weyland, 2013). \\ \midrule

Hopfield Neural Network (HNN) & Hopfield \& Tank (1985) & Canonical & NCO: Improvement Heuristic (UL) & Single-point, General & Energy minimization & Iteratively refines a continuous state relaxation by minimizing an unsupervised energy landscape. & Foundational archetype of unsupervised neural optimization; relies on statistical physics. \\ \midrule

Hybrid Genetic Search (HGS) & Vidal et al. (2012) & Memetic Algorithm & Memetic Algorithm & Population, Routing & Swap, 2-opt, Cross-exchange & GA that applies local search to offspring prior to fitness evaluation. & State-of-the-art framework for routing optimization; transparent engineering. \\ \midrule

Hybrid Volleyball Premier League (HVPL) & Sun et al. (2023) & VPL + ACO + ALNS & Memetic Algorithm (Swarm + Traj.) & Population, Routing & Pheromones, Ruin \& Recreate & Merges the VPL competition framework with ACO routing and ALNS neighborhood search. & \textbf{Exposes the Metaphor:} VPL was too weak to solve hard LRPSPDTW routing constraints. The authors had to inject rigorous, canonical ACO and ALNS to actually perform the search. \\ \midrule

Knowledge Guided Local Search (KGLS) & Arnold \& Sörensen (2018) & GLS & Domain-Specific Meta-heuristic & Single-point, Combinatorial & Steepest descent & Uses data mining to group variables prior to deterministic local search. & Data-driven execution of GLS; precise, honest, and completely devoid of metaphor. \\ \midrule

League Championship Algorithm (LCA) & Kashan (2014) & GA / ES & Relabeled Meta-heuristic (Evol.) & Population, Continuous & Artificial SWOT Analysis & Models a sports schedule where teams adapt "formations" based on win/loss fitness. & \textbf{Deceptive.} Functionally an exhaustive, deterministic steady-state Genetic Algorithm obscured by convoluted sports schedule terminology. \\ \midrule

Lin-Kernighan-Helsgaun (LKH) & Helsgaun (2000) & Lin-Kernighan (1973) & Domain-Specific Heuristic (Traj.) & Single-point, TSP / Routing & Dynamic $k$-opt swap & Searches for improving $k$-opt moves by restricting the search space via minimum spanning trees. & The pinnacle of TSP trajectory heuristics; purely mathematical, completely devoid of metaphor. \\ \midrule

Local Search (LS) / Neighborhood Search & Generic (Croes 1958) & Canonical & Canonical Heuristic (Traj.) & Single-point, General & $k$-opt, swap, insert & Iteratively moves to a strictly improving neighboring solution until a local optimum is reached. & The absolute foundational trajectory-based optimization mechanism. \\ \midrule

Metropolis-Hastings (MH) & Metropolis et al. (1953) & Canonical & Statistical Acceptance & Single-point, General & Random perturbation & Accepts moves based on probability density ratios to sample from a distribution. & The core statistical mechanics foundation from physics that directly birthed SA. \\ \midrule

NeuRewriter & Chen et al. (2019) & LS + RL & NCO: Improvement Heuristic (RL) & Single-point, Combinatorial & MDP rewriting (swap/insert) & Uses an actor-critic RL framework to iteratively refine a solution by selecting rewrite rules. & Directly maps classical trajectory search into an RL framework. \\ \midrule

Physics-Inspired GNN (PI-GNN) & Schuetz et al. (2022) & GNN + QUBO & NCO: Non-AR Constructive (UL) & Single-point, Combinatorial & QUBO energy formulation & Learns soft node assignments in one step by directly minimizing the physics-based Hamiltonian energy. & Modern Unsupervised Learning. Replaces heuristic search with continuous energy gradients. \\ \midrule

Pointer Network (Ptr-Net) & Vinyals et al. (2015) & Seq2Seq & NCO: Autoregressive Constructive (SL) & Sequence, Combinatorial & Attention mechanism & Uses an LSTM with attention to output a permutation of the input graph nodes step-by-step. & Foundational SL model that birthed NCO. Limited by reliance on optimal labels. \\ \midrule

Reinforcement Learning B\&B (RL-B\&B) & Sun et al. (2020) & RL + B\&B & NCO: Predict-Many (RL) & Complete Tree, MILP & RL-based variable branching & Interacts with the B\&B tree as an environment to minimize tree size via RL actions. & Rigorous integration of RL into exact mathematical programming spaces. \\ \midrule

Ruin and Recreate (R\&R) & Schrimpf et al. (2000) & LNS & Canonical Meta-heuristic (Traj.) & Single-point, Combinatorial & Destruction/Construction & Alternates between partially destroying a solution and deterministically rebuilding it. & Foundational paradigm shifting away from minor perturbations to massive structural repairs. \\ \midrule

Simulated Annealing (SA) & Kirkpatrick (1983) & MH & Canonical Meta-heuristic & Single-point, General & Probabilistic acceptance & Probabilistically accepts worse solutions based on a decreasing temperature $T$. & Foundational link between statistical mechanics and combinatorial optimization. \\ \midrule

Simulated Annealing Neighborhood Search & Jorge et al. (2022) & SA + LS & Hybrid Meta-heuristic & Single-point, Routing & Inter/intra-route swaps & Explores diverse neighborhood structures while utilizing SA's cooling schedule. & Transparent hybridization; correctly attributes its mathematical foundations. \\ \midrule

Sine Cosine Algorithm (SCA) & Mirjalili (2016) & PSO / DE & Relabeled Meta-heuristic (Swarm) & Swarm, Continuous & Trigonometric updates & Fluctuates search agents toward/away from best solution using sine/cosine functions. & \textbf{Deceptive.} Meaningless trigonometric "math-washing" applied to standard vector translation operations. \\ \midrule

Slack Induction by String Removal (SISR) & Christiaens (2020) & R\&R & Domain-Specific Meta-heuristic & Single-point, Routing & String Removal & Removes spatial sequences of variables and reinserts them to minimize slack. & Genuinely novel spatial meta-heuristic; achieved state-of-the-art results in large-scale routing. \\ \midrule

Soccer League Competition (SLC) & Moosavian \& Roodsari (2014) & GA & Relabeled Meta-heuristic (Evol.) & Population, Continuous & Substitutes (stochastic restarts) & Simulates matches, substitute players, and league standings to optimize real-valued networks. & \textbf{Deceptive.} Convoluted, inefficient multi-population Genetic Algorithm burdened with unnecessary sports rule constraints. \\ \midrule

TSP-GCN & Joshi et al. (2019) & GNN & NCO: Non-AR Constructive (SL) & Edge Probabilities, Routing & Supervised classification & SL model learns to output the entire solution probability distribution in a single step. & Foundational Non-AR baseline. Fast inference, but lacks autoregressive tracking. \\ \midrule

Variable Neighborhood Search (VNS) & Mladenović (1997) & LS & Canonical Meta-heuristic & Single-point, Combinatorial & Nested neighborhoods & Systematically switches neighborhoods when trapped in local optima. & Foundational framework establishing rules for trajectory diversification. \\ \midrule

Volleyball Premier League (VPL) & Moghdani \& Salimifard (2018) & GA / ES & Relabeled Meta-heuristic (Evol.) & Population, Continuous & Substitution (uniform mutation) & Mimics volleyball coaching and interactions via substitution, coaching, and learning phases. & \textbf{Deceptive.} Fundamentally flawed GA/ES hybrid that relies on overly constrained, thematic "coaching" phases instead of rigorous exploitation. \\ \midrule

Whale Optimization Algorithm (WOA) & Mirjalili \& Lewis (2016) & PSO / DE & Relabeled Meta-heuristic (Swarm) & Swarm, Continuous & Logarithmic spirals & Models bubble-net hunting using spiral vector updating mechanisms. & \textbf{Deceptive.} Obfuscates standard trajectory math behind aquatic terminology. \\
